About

Engineering leader with 25+ years in software and the last decade in AI. Most recently CTO at ThirdLaw, building runtime AI safety for the enterprise. Founded Nervana's software team and grew it from 10 to 150 engineers across four countries after Intel's acquisition, and founded and sold Luminide.

Strongest as a VP of Engineering — strategy, recruiting, and org-building — and hands-on daily with agentic engineering. Computer Science Ph.D., two patents, and 20+ publications including Flexpoint at NeurIPS.
